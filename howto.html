How to use the system


1

Register your canteen 

When you register a canteen you will be the admin for that canteen and will have the ability to preview /edit the user database for that canteen including adding balance to the user's account and updating the food database i.e adding and updating the food menu.

2

Register as a user

When you register as a user you will have the option to register for canteen(s) from the canteen list.

3

Load balance and start ordering

After the registration you will have to load your account's balance through the admin. Once that is done you can start ordering from anywhere.
